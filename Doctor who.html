Doctor who

By Fernando Osborn (Nando Perry Perry Chicken)

Doctor who is a Brittish television show invented in the 1900's and continued on till now.

As the doctor is a time travelling alien from Gallifrey, when he dies, he regenerates into a

different person. This means that he never dies but his personality and appearance changes.

The doctor has alien technology called a 'TARDIS'. This stands for Time And Relative Dimensions In Space.

The doctor's TARDIS can travel through time and space. Not only can the TARDIS do all of these things,

but it is also bigger on the inside and can produce technology. Every episode, there is an alien threat that the doctor

needs to thwart for the safety of the human race.

The war doctor:

Played by:

John Hurt

Companions:

None.

A bit about him:

The war doctor is the regeneration after the 8th doctor. He is angry and wants to destroy

the daleks and save all of his people. He tries to end the suffering but does not succeed.

Eventually, he steals a piece of time lord technology called 'the moment' and if he pushes

the button, he will end the suffering by killing everyone on the planet (including the time lords)

but him. This would mean that he would end the suffering but kill billions of people.

It seems as though he pushed the button, but as later told in 'the day of the doctor'

a 50th aniversary episode of Doctor who, the 11th doctor, the 10th and the war doctor

all team up and try to defeat zygons. Just before he initiates the 'moment', the other

doctors come up to him and stop him from doing it with the help of Clara (a future companion)

At the end of the episode, all of the doctors unite and pull Gallifrey and all of the

time lords (not the daleks) into an alternate dimension where they are safe but no one

can find them. This means that he did not kill all of those people, but saved them all from eternal

suffering. Because of the time line, his future regenerations do not remember this

(excluding 11th onwards) so they still feel regret and that regret, as the war doctor puts it,

saves billions of galaxies and lives.
